Frequently Asked Questions

The Sanford branch lobby and drive-up are open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM.
Yes, the Kennebunk Savings Sanford branch at 1314 Main Street has a 24-hour ATM available for deposits and withdrawals.
Yes, the Sanford branch offers drive-up banking Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM.
