Community Banking in Southern Maine and New Hampshire

Kennebunk Savings Bank has been a community bank serving York County, Maine and the New Hampshire Seacoast since 1871. With 12 branches in Maine and 4 branches in New Hampshire, you're never far from a location that offers checking accounts, savings accounts, mortgages, home equity loans, personal loans, and business banking.

Our branches are in Berwick, Eliot, Kennebunk, Kittery, North Berwick, Ogunquit, Sanford, Wells, and York in Maine, plus Dover, Hampton, Portsmouth, and Stratham in New Hampshire. Each location has an ATM, and most offer drive-up banking and Saturday hours so you can bank on your schedule.

What You Can Do at Any Branch

  • Open a personal checking or savings account
  • Apply for a mortgage, home equity loan, or personal loan
  • Set up business accounts and cash management
  • Get help with online and mobile banking
  • Talk to an insurance advisor about home, auto, or life coverage
  • Use the ATM for deposits and withdrawals around the clock

Banking Hours and Availability

Most Kennebunk Savings branches open at 9:00 AM on weekdays. Some locations close at 4:30 PM Monday through Thursday with extended hours on Friday, while others stay open until 5:00 PM all week. Saturday lobby hours are available at most branches from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Our Customer Care Team is available Monday through Thursday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:30 PM, and Saturday from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM at 1-800-339-6573.

Serving Cumberland, York, Rockingham, and Strafford Counties

Bank and lending products and services are available to customers who live or work in Cumberland or York Counties in Maine, or Rockingham or Strafford Counties in New Hampshire. Whether you're in Kennebunk, Kittery, Dover, or Portsmouth, there's a Kennebunk Savings branch nearby with the personal attention you'd expect from a community bank that gives 10% of its earnings back to local nonprofits every year.
